About Ulelero Farms

Your trusted partner in poultry.

Our Mission & Vision

Our vision is to be Rumphi’s most reliable supplier of safe, affordable chicken products. We are on a mission to deliver fresh, hygienically-handled poultry while improving rural livelihoods and promoting soil health through the recycling of poultry manure.

Quality: Consistently delivering healthy, well-sized birds.
Biosecurity: Upholding the strictest hygiene standards to ensure safe products.
Customer Focus: Meeting your needs with reliable supply and excellent service.

Our Products

Fresh from our farm to your table.

Live Broilers

Healthy, fast-growing broilers, perfect for those who prefer to process their own chicken. Ideal for quick cash flow and fresh consumption.

Dressed Whole Birds

Hygienically processed and chilled or frozen for your convenience. Available in consistent weight bands (1.2–1.6 kg).

Premium Chicken Parts

Conveniently packed wings, drumsticks, thighs, and breasts. Perfect for specific recipes and portion control.

Composted Manure

A valuable by-product for agriculture. Our rich, composted poultry manure improves soil health and crop yields.

Our Quality Promise

What makes Ulelero Farms different.

Strict Biosecurity

We enforce rigorous health and safety protocols, including visitor logs, footbaths, and pest control, to prevent disease and ensure a healthy flock.

Optimal Nutrition & Care

Our birds receive a balanced diet (Starter, Grower, Finisher) and clean, sanitized water to achieve an excellent feed conversion ratio and healthy growth.

Reliable & Consistent

We solve the problem of stock-outs and inconsistent quality. With us, you get predictable sizing, reliable delivery, and the option to pre-order with deposits.
